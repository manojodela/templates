Wisdom Series
 
 
There will be a new series of programs with multiple episodes of inspiring thoughts and practical advice from several Yoga practitioners and Yoga experts from all over the world. With them, we will revisit the timeless philosophy of Yoga and the universal techniques that one can experience.
 
  • Sept - Oct: Exploring the Bhagavad Gita - Wisdom sessions - Philosophy, management, setting goals: the influence of the Bhagavad Gita in our professional and family life and more.

  • Nov - Dec: Exploring Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras - The mind and Emotions - Yoga and psychology: tools for a better management of our emotions and our mind.

  • Jan - Feb: Exploring Yoga techniques - The Hatha Yoga Pradipika - Introduction to Ayurveda, Lifestyle and diet

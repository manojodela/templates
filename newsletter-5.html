Saturday Sessions
 
 
We started a new series of interviews to invite you all to explore ancient texts of Yoga that make the basis of all Yoga practices and philosophy. This series is called ‘Living Waters’ and the teachings are practical tips that one can apply in everyday life. We have interviewed people from different background who have one thing in common: the Bhagavad Gita has given them answers in their daily dealings, at home or at work. They have generously shared with us.
 
 
 
 
Episode 1: Message for the Youth
 
 
This week let us listen to Aryamba Sriram, a young bharatanatyam dancer; Ranjani Manian, a successful business woman who has spent her life helping overseas settle in India and shared with them the immense heritage of Indian Culture; Dr. Sanjay Malpani, an educator and business man who is the president of the Geeta Pariwar, teaching values through the Bhagavad Gita to children across India; and Vani Kola, founder and managing director of Kalaari Capital, an Indian venture capital firm.

The “Meditate with Daaji” program gives us valuable life lessons, shows us how to meditate, and apply in a practical way the wisdom of yogic philosophy as per the Heartfulness way. Whether you are beginners or advanced meditation practitioners, these skills are meant for all. He answers questions by several viewers and meditation enthusiasts in these sessions. To ask questions to Daaji regarding Heartfulness and Yoga kindly write to : yoga4unity@heartfulness.org.

HELP

Heartfulness Experience Life’s Potential

The HELP Program For BIE Telangana Students Academic year 2021-2022

Heartfulness presents

A 20-session certificate course for students on life-skills and holistic well-being through Heartfulness practices for self-development and inner-excellence. Telangana Board of Intermediate Education and Heartfulness Education Trust are collaborating in offering the HELP program to the BIE students of Telangan. Given the current pandemic situation, we are offering our HELP program completely online ensuring equal effectiveness through interactions and experiential learning. The proposal is to offer the HELP program completely free of charge to 1-lakh BIE students of Govt. and Govt. Aided colleges of Telangana over the entire duration of the academic year 2021-2022.

ABOUT US

Heartfulness Education Trust (HET) was born from the vision that the most effective way to transform society in a generation is to provide teachers with the opportunity and tools for change and empower them to transform their students. HET comes under the umbrella of a group of organizations, including Shri Ram Chandra Mission (SRCM), Sahaj Marg Spirituality Foundation (SMSF), and Heartfulness Institute (HFI). Together they work towards social transformation by offering Heartfulness methods for individual change. The Heartfulness education programs are completely secular and “Evidence Based” programs with a basis on scientific approach and direct perception for holistic growth and overall well-being. As of February 2021, the HELP program (Heartfulness: Experience Life’s Potential) for students of grades 10 to 12 has already covered over 500,000 students across the country and is designed and positioned to scale in a very large way over the coming years.

Course Outline

Every HELP session will cover a unique topic on life-skills and well-being, followed by practical experiential session on Heartfulness tools and techniques. A typical session breakup is as follows:

Topic-related presentation and interaction

Experiential session on Heartfulness Meditation tools

Q&A and follow-up

Topic-related presentations will be guided by high-quality presentations and video content on these various topics. Topics will include various elements of science, activities, stories, quotes and morals, which are built into the presentations. The content will equip the presenter with interactive tools to engage with the students and communicate the message in a subtle yet powerful way, where the students learn through interactions, surveys and discussions.

Impact Assesment

HELP is an "evidence-based” program founded in science and research with defined outcomes and impact. Monitoring and evaluation are done with baseline and end-line measurements to determine the effect of the program on specific qualities of the students. Monitoring will be done using questionnaires, interviews, in-depth case studies, and additional psychometric tools. The research article published in IJDR1 indicates how the overall student’s self-esteem levels had increased significantly ( < 0.05) after the intervention of HELP program. About Heartfulness Education Trust (HET)

Heartfulness Education Trust (HET) was born with the vision to enable teachers and students to live a balanced, purposeful, and a happy life using heart-based practices, so that they are equipped to contribute towards a sustainable school reform and community citizenship. Our self-development programs in schools and colleges are creating a culture of values for both teachers and students, through practical methods of relaxation, introspection and meditation. Heartfulness has centers in over 130 countries, serving more than one million people worldwide.
